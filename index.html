Hi Cy! first of all, happy happy birthday! Seryoso, deserve mo lahat ng love, saya, at blessings today at sa araw-araw rin. Ang dami mong pinapakita na strength at effort, kahit minsan feeling mo hindi enough, I want you to know na you’re already doing such a good job and for that, I am proud of youu :))

Proud ako sa’yo, Cy, dahil sa lahat ng bagay na na-achieve mo, kahit ‘yung mga simpleng bagay na hindi mo napapansin, malaking bagay ‘yon. And the way you handle things kahit may pagsubok, grabe, ang lakas mo. Pero Cy, reminder lang din na hindi mo kailangang palaging maging malakas, okay lang mapagod at okay lang magpahinga.

Be gentle with yourself, Cy. Hindi mo kailangan i-rush lahat, hindi mo rin kailangang i-pressure sarili mo palagi. Sapat ka na at sobra pa nga. Also, stay hydrated, please? Alam ko lagi kong sinasabi pero seryoso, importante ‘yan. Huwag puro softdrinks at mag ka tubig din. Don’t pressure yourself masyado coz life is not a race. Take your time, step by step. May tamang timing para sa lahat. And always remember, it’s okay to rest. Hindi selfish ang magpahinga, hindi ka mahina kapag nagpapa-heal ka. You deserve peace, love, and happiness.

Sana habang binabasa mo ‘to, napapangiti ka, kasi you deserve to smile not just today but everyday. Alam kong minsan nakakapagod, minsan parang kulang, pero Cy, you’re more than enough. Andito ako lagi, cheerleader mo at kakampi mo forever.

So today, enjoy your special day, Cy! Kumain ka at magpakabusog, tumawa ka nang malakas, gawin mo lahat ng nagpapasaya sa’yo. You deserve to celebrate yourself kasi you’re such a blessing sa lahat ng tao sa paligid mo.

Happy birthday ulit, Cyrean! Sana this year magdala sa’yo ng mas maraming reasons to smile, more peace of mind, more love, at more moments na marerealize mo how special you really are.
